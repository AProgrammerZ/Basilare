How To Cook Steak

Reverse Sear Method:

  1. Preheat oven to ___
  2. Place steak on a cooling rack over a baking tray, and put into oven
  3. Cook for ____
  4. Remove steak with cooling rack and tray
  5. Preheat a pan to medium high heat on the stove
  6. Sear the steak for one minute on each side
  7. Sear the sides of the steak for 30 seconds
  8. No need to rest the steak with this method, so eat relatively immediately

Regardless of Method Used, Make Sure to Follow these Tips:

  • Let the steak come to about room temperature before cooking
  • Dry the steak thoroughly before cooking
  • Salt the steak either within minutes before cooking or ___
  • Don't season steak until the end of cooking process to prevent burning of seasoning

Sear then Oven Method:

  1. Preheat oven to ___
  2. Preheat pan to medium high heat
  3. Sear the steak for one minute on each side
  4. Sear the sides of the steak for 30 seconds
  5. Put pan with steak into oven and cook for___
  6. Let steak rest, covered, for __ minutes before eating

Flipping Method:

  1. Preheat pan to medium high heat
  2. Cook steak for __ minutes, flipping every minute or less
  3. Let steak rest, covered, for __ minutes before eating
